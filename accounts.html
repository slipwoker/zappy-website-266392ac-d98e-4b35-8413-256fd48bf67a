Common account questions

Everything you need to know about getting started, managing your account, and scaling your trading with PrimeGlobalFX. We've answered the questions our traders ask most often so you can move forward with confidence.

What is the minimum deposit to open an account?

You can start trading with as little as $1,000 on our Basic tier. Each subsequent tier unlocks tighter spreads, higher leverage, and dedicated support. We recommend funding at a level that aligns with your trading goals and risk strategy.

How does leverage access work across account types?

Leverage scales with your account tier — from 1:100 on Basic up to 1:500 on Platinum. Higher leverage amplifies your market exposure, but we always encourage responsible use and provide risk-management tools to protect your capital.

What is the account upgrade path?

You can upgrade at any time by funding the difference to the next tier. Upgrades unlock lower spreads, a personal mentor (Silver and above), VIP signals, and priority withdrawals. Your account manager will guide you through the process seamlessly.

Who receives dedicated mentor support?

Personal mentor support begins at the Silver tier and becomes a dedicated VIP manager at Gold and Platinum levels. Your mentor helps you refine strategy, interpret market signals, and stay disciplined — giving you an edge from day one.

What assets can I trade on the mt4 forex trading platform?

PrimeGlobalFX offers 200+ instruments across forex, indices, commodities, shares, and cryptocurrencies — all accessible through the industry-standard MT4 platform. Enjoy fast execution, deep liquidity, and real-time charting on every asset class.

How do account recommendations work?

Our interactive onboarding quiz evaluates your investment level, preferred assets, experience, and goals to recommend the account tier best suited for you. The recommendation is unbiased — it's designed to match you with the right tools, not the highest deposit.
